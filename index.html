Food charity is appreciated but entirely your choice. It is optional, not mandatory.

You can contribute food to those in need as a way to celebrate your or your family’s special events like Birthdays or wedding anniversaries, and receive blessings in return.

Recommending specific organizations or individuals for donations goes against the guidelines of our community.
