About Us

"Food for Hunger" is a voluntary initiative that serves fresh meals and water bottles to people in need тАФ especially on meaningful days like birthdays, anniversaries, or religious occasions. In the past 3 years, over 100,000 meals have been shared with love and care.

Started by Mrs. Kavitha Prabhu, a compassionate socialist, our mission is simple: help others and spread joy through food.
